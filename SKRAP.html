SKRAP

Skrap is an environment sustainability firm that helps businesses and brands adopt sustainable practices and zero waste solutions. Our aim is to build awareness around 'waste' and inspire positive climate action.

Mumbai-based Skrap provides an end-to-end process of waste management at events and offices. Founded in March 2017, they analyse waste generation data based on the type and size of event, devise a customised plan and implement it on the premises.The idea is to reduce waste significantly, and recycle a huge mass of what’s been produced. At the YouTube FanFest in March 2018, Skrap recycled, composted or reused almost 80 per cent of the four tonnes of waste generated.
